PROFILE OF BIHAR

 
CHILD RIGHTS
LINKS
 

North Bihar is one of the major source areas for trafficking of children and for forced labour. Organized crime groups have cropped up and are taking advantage of situation of vulnerability by trafficking scores of children and adolescents. The IKARD works to undertake collective action for promotion and protection of children’s rights. The organization has been working towards promotion and protection of children’s rights by ensuring formal education in child labour special schools, raise awareness among public and government officials about Care and Protection of Children (Juvenile justice Act), counseling services, and rescue operations.

The IKARD focuses on following thematic areas:-

Child labour
Child trafficking
Education and early childhood development
Children infected and affected by HIV/AIDS
Children with disabilities
Children affected by armed conflict.
Child sexual abuse and exploitation.
